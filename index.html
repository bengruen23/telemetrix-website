About Telemetrix

The companies building our resilient future don't get enough airtime. While everyone talks about consumer AI, the real action is in infrastructure — the systems that will determine which countries, companies, and communities thrive in an increasingly uncertain world.

Telemetrix is where these builders tell their stories. We cover the full spectrum: AI infrastructure that powers autonomous systems, energy technologies that reduce dependence on unstable grids, defense innovations that protect critical assets, supply chain tools that prevent disruptions, and the underlying systems that make modern life possible.

Every conversation is designed to extract insights you won't hear elsewhere — the technical details, market dynamics, and strategic thinking behind the most important technologies being built today.
